THIS IS STREETMEET

StreetMeet is a community driven organization founded March 2015 in Washington, D.C. Our mission is to bring together communities of diverse creators through free and paid networking and workshop events.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

We don't like to complicate things. We schedule meetups on a monthly basis for creatives of all levels to link up and create.

Each month we provide a FREE meetup to attend. With the exception of special events, we want this experience to be as accessible to as many people as possible.

Once at the meet, the rules and guidelines will be laid out by the hosts. For the most part it's simple — show up, meet people, create some cool content together, and if you're lucky, you may make some awesome friends along the way!
