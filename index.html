About Us

We're dedicated to connecting photographers, models, and videographers through accessible, inclusive events. We exist to lower the barriers to creative collaboration — and to help talented people find their people.

How It Works

Each month, StreetMeet hosts a free meetup open to creatives at every experience level. Our events are intentionally accessible — no membership fees, no prerequisites, just a shared interest in creating.

When you arrive, your host will walk you through the format for the day. From there, it's about meeting fellow creatives, collaborating in real time, and leaving with new connections and new work.
